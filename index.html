About

This is the journey we have developed together.

  • Nov 2020

    This is our first time working together as a team

    We met at the cafe and got the first bug bounty in 30 minutes after submitting for 8x8-bounty, it was amazing!

  • 2021

    We're still here but the number of members has changed a lot

    Being a bug bounty hunter is not easy, stable and always is a controversy topic, so some members have their own directions.

  • 2022

    We start to operate professionally periodically

    We started to operate more professionally. Having a clear working time, space, target, and established rules helps make operations more effective.

  • Feb 2023

    Flysec created

    At this time, the name Flysec was coined, “Fly” simply means flying higher and farther, “Sec” is Security in Cyber Security.
